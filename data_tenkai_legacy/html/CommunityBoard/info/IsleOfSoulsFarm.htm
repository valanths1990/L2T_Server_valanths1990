Isle Of Souls

There the monsters are weak and you can farm Silver Shilen and Unbind Scrolls.



Nornil's Cave

The Nornil's Cave which is near Stronghold III contains three mysterious rooms where the Nornil's Manifest can be activated.
Around the Nornil's Cave you can get Silver Shilen, Scroll of Enchant R-Grade and Element Stones.
The Nornil's Manifest will cause the apparition of the  Spicula Larvas.
