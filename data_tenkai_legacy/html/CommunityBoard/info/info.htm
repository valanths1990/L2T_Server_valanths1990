Rates Info

- XP Rate: 1x (Custom exp table) - SP Rate: 20.000x - Adena Rate: 5x (Custom shop prices) - Drop Rate: 1x - Spoil Rate: 1x - There's no level cap
Enchant Info

- Safe Enchant: +16 - Max Enchant: +16 - You can reach enchants over +16 without any limit through a custom multisell
Merchant NPC:

All the purchaseable items can be found in that NPC, which is organized by item categories.
Teleporter NPC

This NPC teleports you practically anywere and for free.
Services NPC

There you will be able to use warehouses, create clans and allies, create any of your available subclasses, bestow SA and augments on your items, draw symbols, delevel...
Magic gem

You will be able to spawn the teleporter and the buffer NPCs anywhere by using that item. Everyone will start with a magic gem and there will be no way to lose it.
Deck

Double clicking it with a monster targeted will give to you his drops and chances which are calculated according to your current level. Everyone will start with a deck and there will be no way to lose it.
