In this section you will find various items being auctioned. If their starting price is in Adena, but you can override any Adena bid by using Coins of Luck.
The auctions have a dynamic remaining time. It will go down normally until less than 1 hour is left for the auction to end. After that, if someone bids, the remaining time will grow to 1h again. Summarizing, the best bid must stay there for at least 1h without being broken.
Good luck!
